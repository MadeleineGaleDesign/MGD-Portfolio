I pride myself on creating high quality designs in addition to thoughtful UX.

My current role requires a wide range of skills; UX/UI responsive design for web & e-commerce, working closely with clients and their brand identity, as well as leading the design team and mentoring junior members of staff. This also includes communicating and leading calls with clients and stakeholders, walking them through the design decisions that align with their creative and functional requirements.

My past experience with textile design built solid design principle understanding. I broadened my knowledge with more digital mediums by studying for an MA Digital Media Arts. I love to bring skills learnt from textile design such as an understanding of colour, spacing and more abstract elements, as well as an eye for detail and design into the web design process, which has enabled me to produce powerful, beautiful designs, giving each client and project something extra.
