Bacardi

Concept

I successfully took the lead on the design process for the new Bacardi brand “Plume & Petal”, a premium low-alcohol percentage vodka for sophisticated working women. I also worked with NWTs to create an experience consistent with other Bacardi brands.

Requirements & Skills

Throughout this project I worked closely with the client, adhering to their vision of creating UX similar to some of their previous sites while tailoring to the specific needs of Plume & Petal.

I worked closely with the brand guidelines to create a very on brand design. I designed a landing page, age gated content, the homepage, products pages and cocktail recipe pages, as well as a store locator with the same functionality as previous sites. In addition I designed creating a whole new experience unique to Plume & Petal called the “Spritz Society”, which was a range of brand ambassadors across America. They also had “Journal” content which was a blog as well as recipes which shows the products in use.
