History Story Time

Concept

A fun historical podcast for kids, they are branching out and selling themed toy subscription boxes with items inside. He wanted a site to sell the toy box, as well as promote his podcast. I chose a functionality (name for flicking through) to emulate a story book for kids, each "page" is a new feature

The client had no brand guidelines, so I defined the visual direction to suit his illustrations and brand. This was a basic shopify theme build, with one bespoke homepage and theme sidecart.

The functionality of the site was inspired by a childrens book, using full height & width immersive screens and a vertical carousel I achieved this effect.
