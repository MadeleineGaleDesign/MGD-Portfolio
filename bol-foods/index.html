Bol Foods

Concept

A large plant based food brand in the UK. They originally had an online store, but wanted more e-commerce optimisation, migration to shopify and make the web and mobile experience feel more modern.

I led the design, and worked closely with the client throughout the project to ensure I understood their requirements and pain-points with their old site in order to solve these problems in a way that would benefit both them and the user. As this project was a bespoke shopify build, all pages on the site were designed and built to be custom.

Designs Desktop

Client Requirements

• Creating a new site structure and design that was much more optimised for ecommerce.

• Communicating their branding in a much more optimised format for desktop and mobile.

• Clearly signposting what is available online vs offline, where users can buy if offline - very important to the client and one of their main pain points

• Another of the clients pain points - making the site much easier to navigate and "less frustrating and confusing" - especially as they wanted their site to be more paid traffic rather than organic so needs to be easy to navigate
